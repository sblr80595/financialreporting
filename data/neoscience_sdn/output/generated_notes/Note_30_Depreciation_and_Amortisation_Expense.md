### Notes and Explanations:

**1. Depreciation on property, plant and equipment (₹7,37,490)** comprises:
   - **GL 6011/00000 - Depreciation of Lab Equipment**: ₹6,89,193
     - This represents the major component of depreciation, primarily relating to laboratory equipment used in operations
   - **GL 9D01/00000 - Depreciation**: ₹48,297
     - This represents general depreciation on other property, plant and equipment

   Both these GLs are classified under the same Ind AS Minor category: "Depreciation on property, plant and equipment (refer note 4)"

**2. Depreciation on right of use assets (₹30,778)** comprises:
   - **Dep on ROU**: ₹30,778
     - This represents depreciation on Right-of-Use (ROU) assets recognized under Ind AS 116 - Leases
     - The ROU asset cost is ₹2,69,429 with accumulated depreciation of ₹1,22,641 as at June 2025

**3. Important Exclusion:**
   - **GL 9D01/00001 - Impairment of Assets**: This GL has been correctly excluded from this note as impairment is not a depreciation expense. Impairment losses are recognized separately as exceptional items in the Statement of Profit and Loss in accordance with Ind AS 36 - Impairment of Assets.

**4. Cross-references:**
   - Refer to **Note 4A** for detailed movement and carrying amounts of Property, Plant and Equipment
   - Refer to **Note 4B** for detailed movement and carrying amounts of Right of Use Assets
   - Refer to **Note 32** for Finance Costs which includes Interest on Lease Liability of ₹1,500

**5. Schedule III Compliance:**
   - This note has been prepared in accordance with Division II of Schedule III to the Companies Act, 2013
   - Depreciation and amortisation represent systematic allocation of depreciable amounts over the useful lives of assets
   - These are non-cash expenses that reduce the carrying value of assets over time

**6. Accounting Policy Reference:**
   - Depreciation methods, useful lives and residual values are reviewed at each financial year-end and adjusted prospectively, if appropriate
   - ROU assets are depreciated over the shorter of lease term or useful life of the underlying asset
