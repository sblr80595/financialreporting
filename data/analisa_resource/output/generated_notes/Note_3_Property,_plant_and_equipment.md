## ACCOUNTING CONTEXT

This note presents Property, Plant and Equipment (PPE) in accordance with Ind AS 16 - Property, Plant and Equipment:

1. **Gross Carrying Amount**: Represents the original acquisition cost of tangible fixed assets including:
   - Plant and Machinery (Laboratory Equipment and Tools & Equipment)
   - Computer equipment and hardware
   - Furniture and fixtures
   - Motor vehicles
   - Leasehold improvements (Renovation costs)
   - Office equipment

2. **Accumulated Depreciation**: The cumulative depreciation charged on these assets from the date of acquisition to the reporting date, systematically allocated over their useful lives.

3. **Net Carrying Amount (Net Book Value)**: The amount at which PPE is carried on the Balance Sheet, representing the unexpired cost of the assets (Gross Carrying Amount less Accumulated Depreciation).

The net carrying amount of ₹423,771.91 represents the value of Property, Plant and Equipment as at (Unaudited) Jun'25, which will be reflected in the non-current assets section of the Balance Sheet.
