## ACCOUNTING NOTES:

**Nature of Right-of-Use Assets:**
- Right-of-use assets represent the Company's right to use underlying leased assets for the lease term as per Ind AS 116 (Leases)
- The gross carrying amount represents the initial measurement of ROU assets at the commencement of the lease
- Accumulated depreciation is charged systematically over the shorter of the lease term or useful life of the underlying asset
- The net carrying amount of ₹4,56,972.74 represents the present value of the Company's right to use the leased assets
