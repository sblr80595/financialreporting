### Notes:

Tax expense comprises both current and deferred tax components for the period ended March 31, 2025:

- **Current Tax (RM 5,986,609.00)**: Represents the actual tax liability for the current financial year based on taxable profits determined in accordance with applicable tax regulations in Malaysia.

- **Deferred Tax (RM 1,375,867.00)**: Represents the tax effect of temporary differences between the carrying amounts of assets and liabilities in the financial statements and their respective tax bases.
