### Notes:
This note captures all other operating expenses during the period ended June 2025.

**Legal and professional expenses** (₹97,238): Includes provision for input VAT (₹5,400), professional fees (₹91,838), and other legal consultation charges. These expenses relate to professional services obtained during the period including audit, tax advisory, and other consultancy services.

**Bank charges** (₹10): Represents banking service charges, transaction fees, and other bank-related expenses incurred during the period.

**Rent** (₹15,000): Comprises rental expenses for premises paid to related parties for operational spaces used by the company.

**Miscellaneous expenses** (₹8,052): Includes sundry operating expenses and other expenses not specifically classified elsewhere.

All expenses are presented as positive amounts representing debit balances in accordance with Schedule III requirements of the Companies Act, 2013.
