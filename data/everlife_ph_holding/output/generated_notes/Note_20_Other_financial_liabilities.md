### Notes:

**1. Due to a related party (Refer note 21)**

This represents amounts payable to related parties amounting to ₹15,65,986.72. These are financial liabilities arising from accruals denominated in SGD for transactions with related parties. The amounts are recognized at amortized cost as per Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments. Detailed disclosures regarding the nature of related party relationships, terms and conditions, and transactions during the year are provided in Note 21 - Related Party Disclosures.

**2. Accrued expense**

This represents expenses that have been incurred during the period but remain unpaid as at the balance sheet date, amounting to ₹2,23,237.60. These accruals are denominated in USD and include various operational expenses incurred but not yet settled. These liabilities are measured at amortized cost in accordance with Ind AS 109.

---

### Accounting Policy

**Classification and Measurement:**
- Other financial liabilities are classified as financial liabilities measured at amortized cost under Ind AS 109
- These liabilities are initially recognized at fair value, net of transaction costs
- Subsequently measured at amortized cost using the effective interest method
- Derecognized when the obligation is discharged, cancelled or expires

**Nature of Liabilities:**
- These are current financial liabilities expected to be settled within 12 months from the reporting date
- No significant financing component is present in these liabilities
- Foreign currency denominated liabilities are translated at the closing exchange rate
