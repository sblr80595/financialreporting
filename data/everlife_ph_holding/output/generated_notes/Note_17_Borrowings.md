### Nature and Terms of Borrowings:

**Non-Current Borrowings:**
This represents unsecured loan from Everlife Holdings Pte Limited (related party) amounting to ₹243,547,464.00. The loan is repayable after 12 months from the reporting date. Details of terms and conditions, interest rates, and repayment schedule are provided in Note 21 - Related Party Disclosures.

**Current Borrowings:**
This represents advances from stockholder - Everlife Holdings Pte Limited amounting to ₹11,903,999.00. These advances are repayable on demand or within 12 months from the reporting date. Details of terms and conditions are provided in Note 21 - Related Party Disclosures.

---

### Accounting Policy:
This note discloses borrowings (both current and non-current) recognized in the balance sheet as per **Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments**. Borrowings are initially recognized at fair value, net of transaction costs incurred, and subsequently measured at amortized cost using the effective interest rate method.

### Related Party Transactions:
All borrowings disclosed above are from related parties. As required by **Ind AS 24 - Related Party Disclosures**, detailed information about the nature of relationships, terms and conditions, interest rates, and outstanding balances is provided in Note 21.

### Schedule III Compliance:
This note is prepared in accordance with Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013, which requires separate disclosure of:
- Secured and unsecured borrowings
- Current and non-current classification
- Nature of borrowings and security provided (if any)
- Cross-reference to related party disclosures
