## NOTES:

1. **Raw Material Consumption**: The company has consumed raw materials during the period, comprising both imported and indigenous sources. The imported purchases amount to ₹44,59,612.98, while indigenous purchases total ₹30,48,942.50.

3. **Foreign Currency Earnings**: The company earned foreign currency through export sales of ₹9,25,423.06 and export incentives of ₹13,057.47 (comprising Duty Drawback of ₹9,216.79 and Rodtap incentive of ₹3,840.68), resulting in total foreign currency earnings of ₹9,38,480.53.
