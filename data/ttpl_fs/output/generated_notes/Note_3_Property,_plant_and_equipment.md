## ACCOUNTING CONTEXT

This note presents the Property, Plant and Equipment (PPE) of the Company in accordance with Ind AS 16 - Property, Plant and Equipment. The disclosure shows:

1. **Gross Carrying Amount (Cost)**: Represents the original acquisition cost of tangible fixed assets including:
   - Building (₹981.87 lakhs)
   - Plant and equipment (₹1,648.50 lakhs) - primarily Plant & Machinery
   - Computers and accessories (₹272.23 lakhs)
   - Furniture and fixtures (₹282.75 lakhs)
   - Office equipments (₹72.16 lakhs)
   - Vehicles (₹339.36 lakhs)
   - Leasehold improvements (₹351.17 lakhs)

2. **Accumulated Depreciation**: Total depreciation charged on these assets from the date of acquisition to the reporting date amounts to ₹1,566.83 lakhs, representing the systematic allocation of the depreciable amount over the useful life of the assets.

3. **Net Carrying Amount (Net Book Value)**: The balance of ₹2,381.21 lakhs represents the amount at which PPE is carried on the Balance Sheet after deducting accumulated depreciation from gross carrying amount. This is the recoverable value of the Company's tangible fixed assets as at June 30, 2025.

The depreciation on these assets is charged to the Statement of Profit and Loss on a systematic basis over the estimated useful life of the assets as per the Company's accounting policy and Schedule II of the Companies Act, 2013.
