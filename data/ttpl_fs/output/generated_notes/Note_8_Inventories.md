## ACCOUNTING NOTES:

1. **Valuation Policy**: Inventories are valued at the lower of cost and net realisable value (NRV) in accordance with Ind AS 2 - Inventories.

2. **Cost Determination**: 
   - Cost of raw materials and packaging materials includes purchase price, duties, taxes (non-refundable), and other costs directly attributable to the acquisition.
   - Work-in-progress and finished goods include cost of direct materials, labour, and appropriate allocation of production overheads.

3. **Net Realisable Value**: NRV is the estimated selling price in the ordinary course of business less estimated costs of completion and estimated costs necessary to make the sale.

4. **Provision for Obsolescence**: No provision for inventory obsolescence has been recorded during the period as there are no obsolete or slow-moving inventories identified as at (Unaudited) Jun'25. The GL account "Allowance for obsolete inventories" shows no balance.

5. **Components**:
   - **Raw material and packaging material** (₹2,808.34 lakhs): Includes raw materials, packaging materials, chemicals, consumables, and rejected materials available for production.
   - **Work-in-progress** (₹980.48 lakhs): Represents goods that are in the process of manufacture but not yet completed.
   - **Finished goods** (₹2,641.95 lakhs): Includes manufactured products ready for sale, including rejected finished goods awaiting disposal decision.
   - **Stock-in-trade** (₹2,695.47 lakhs): Represents trading goods purchased for resale.
   - **Stock in transit** (₹2.90 lakhs): Represents goods in transit at the period end.

6. **Inventory Movement**: The net inventory balance of ₹9,129.14 lakhs represents the closing inventory position as at (Unaudited) Jun'25.
