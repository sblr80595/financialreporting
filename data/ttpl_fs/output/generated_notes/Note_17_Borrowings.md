## Notes to the Borrowings Schedule:

### Classification and Measurement
- Borrowings are classified as Non-current (17A) when the obligation is due for settlement after 12 months from the reporting date, and Current (17B) when due within 12 months.
- All borrowings are measured at amortised cost in accordance with Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments.

### Section 17A: Non-current Borrowings
**Secured Borrowings:**
- **Vehicle Loans (₹40.44 lakhs)**: Consists of multiple vehicle loans from HDFC Bank and Kotak Mahindra Prime Ltd secured against respective vehicles. Details of security, interest rates, and terms of repayment are disclosed in note (e) below.

### Section 17B: Current Borrowings
**Secured Borrowings:**
- **Buyers Credit (₹1,565.63 lakhs)**: Foreign currency buyers credit facilities in Euro, secured as per note (a) below.
- **Foreign Currency Working Capital Loan (₹1,514.85 lakhs)**: FCNR loan facility in Euro for working capital requirements, secured as per note (b) below.
- **Cash Credit Limit (₹5,665.99 lakhs)**: Comprises cash credit facilities from HDFC Bank, CITI Bank, and ICICI Bank. Security details provided in notes (a), (b), (c), and (d) below.
- **Term Loan from Bank (₹0.00)**: No outstanding balance as at the reporting date.

**Unsecured Borrowings:**
- **Loan from Holding Company (₹12,906.04 lakhs)**: Unsecured loan from IHL (holding company). Terms and conditions disclosed in note (f) below.

**Current Maturity of Long-term Borrowings:**
- No current maturity component identified for the period under review.

## Accounting Context:
The classification between current and non-current borrowings is based on the contractual maturity period from the Balance Sheet date. Secured borrowings are backed by specific assets or guarantees as detailed in the respective notes. Interest rates on borrowings vary based on the nature of the facility and prevailing market conditions. The Company ensures compliance with all covenants attached to borrowing arrangements.
