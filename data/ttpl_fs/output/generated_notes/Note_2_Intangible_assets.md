### ACCOUNTING CONTEXT AND NOTES:

**Nature of Intangible Assets:**
- **Software (₹132.38 Lakhs):** Represents capitalized software costs acquired or developed for business use.
- **Intangible Assets Under Development (₹634.77 Lakhs):** Represents costs incurred on intangible assets that are not yet ready for their intended use. These assets are not amortised until they are available for use.

**Amortisation Policy:**
- Intangible assets are amortised over their estimated useful lives on a systematic basis.
- As per Ind AS 38, amortisation commences when the asset is available for use.
- The accumulated amortisation of ₹74.17 Lakhs relates entirely to software that has been put to use.
- Intangible assets under development (IAUD) are not amortised until they are completed and ready for use, hence no amortisation is charged on these assets.

**Net Carrying Amount:**
- The net book value of intangible assets as at (Unaudited) Jun'25 is ₹692.98 Lakhs.
- This represents the cost of intangible assets less accumulated amortisation to date.
