### Note Description:

This note discloses cash and cash equivalents recognized in the balance sheet as per Ind AS 7 - Statement of Cash Flows.

**Cash and cash equivalents** comprise cash at bank and on hand, and short-term deposits with an original maturity of three months or less, which are subject to an insignificant risk of changes in value. These balances are readily available for use in the company's operations and meet the definition of cash equivalents under Ind AS 7.

The company maintains bank balances with reputable financial institutions to facilitate day-to-day operational requirements and liquidity management. All bank balances disclosed above are unrestricted and available for immediate use.

**Schedule III Compliance**: This disclosure follows the requirements of Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013 for balance sheet presentation of cash and cash equivalents.

**Ind AS 7 Requirements**: Cash and cash equivalents are measured at their face value as they are readily convertible to known amounts of cash with insignificant risk of changes in value. Original maturity of all balances is less than three months.
