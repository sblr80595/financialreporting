### Note Disclosure:

This note discloses borrowings (both current and non-current) recognized in the balance sheet as per Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments.

**Non-Current Borrowings (Unsecured):**
- **Loan from Related Parties**: Represents unsecured loans from related parties with repayment terms beyond 12 months from the reporting date. These borrowings are typically from holding companies, promoters, or other related entities and are measured at amortized cost. Refer to Note 21 for detailed terms, conditions, interest rates, and repayment schedules.

**Current Borrowings:**
- **Other Borrowings (Advances from stockholders)**: Represents advances and short-term loans received from stockholders/shareholders that are repayable within 12 months or on demand. These amounts are unsecured and include temporary funding support from equity holders. Refer to Note 21 for detailed terms, conditions, and repayment obligations.

**Accounting Policy:**
Borrowings are recognized as financial liabilities and are initially measured at fair value, net of transaction costs. Subsequently, they are measured at amortized cost using the effective interest rate method. Interest expense is recognized on an accrual basis using the effective interest rate method.

**Related Party Information (Ind AS 24):**
All borrowings are from related parties (Everlife Holdings Pte Limited). The nature of relationship, terms and conditions, interest rates, repayment terms, and details of any guarantees are disclosed in Note 21.
