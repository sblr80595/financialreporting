### Note Disclosure:

This note discloses investments recognized in the balance sheet as per Ind AS 28 - Investments in Associates and Joint Ventures.

**Investments in Associates** comprise equity investments in associate companies where the company has significant influence but not control over financial and operating policy decisions. The investment of ₱ 960,000,000.00 represents the company's interest in Lifeline Holdings Inc.

**Accounting Treatment:**
- Investments in associates are initially recognized at cost
- Subsequently measured using the equity method as per Ind AS 28
- The carrying amount reflects adjustments for:
  * Share of post-acquisition profits or losses of the associate
  * Share of other comprehensive income of the associate
  * Dividends received from the associate
  * Any impairment losses recognized

**Schedule III Compliance:**
This format follows Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013 requirements for investments disclosure on the Balance Sheet as a non-current asset.
