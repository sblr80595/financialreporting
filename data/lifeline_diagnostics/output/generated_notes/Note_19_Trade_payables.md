### Notes:

This note discloses trade payables recognized in the balance sheet as per Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments and Schedule III disclosure requirements.

**Trade payables** represent amounts owed to suppliers for goods and services purchased on credit in the normal course of business. These financial liabilities are measured at amortized cost under Ind AS 109.

**Composition of Trade Payables:**
- Foreign suppliers: ₹15,45,50,960.78
- Domestic suppliers: ₹26,32,29,964.79

- Other payables: ₹1,02,69,845.31

**Schedule III Compliance Notes:**
1. Trade payables are presented as current liabilities in the balance sheet
2. These represent amounts payable to suppliers (other than micro and small enterprises)
3. The Company may be required to provide additional disclosures for:
   - Ageing analysis of trade payables
   - Amounts due to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) separately as per MSMED Act
   - Amounts due to related parties, if any
   - Details of payables outstanding beyond normal credit period
4. Trade payables are measured at amortized cost using the effective interest method
5. All amounts are unsecured and non-interest bearing unless otherwise specified
