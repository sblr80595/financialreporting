### Accounting Policy:

This note discloses borrowings (both current and non-current) recognized in the balance sheet as per **Ind AS 109 - Financial Instruments**.

**Recognition and Measurement:**
- All borrowings are initially recognized at fair value, net of transaction costs incurred
- Borrowings are subsequently measured at amortized cost using the effective interest rate method
- Any difference between the proceeds (net of transaction costs) and the redemption value is recognized in the statement of profit and loss over the period of the borrowings

**Classification:**
- Borrowings are classified as current liabilities unless the Company has an unconditional right to defer settlement for at least 12 months after the reporting date
- Current portion of long-term borrowings are separately presented under current borrowings

### Additional Disclosures (Schedule III Compliance):

As per Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013, the following additional information should be disclosed in the complete financial statements:

1. **Nature of Security:** Details of securities provided for secured borrowings
2. **Terms of Repayment:** Specific repayment schedules and maturity profiles
3. **Rate of Interest:** Applicable interest rates for each category of borrowing
4. **Default Information:** Any defaults in repayment of loans and interest
5. **Quarterly Returns:** Reconciliation with quarterly returns filed with banks/financial institutions
