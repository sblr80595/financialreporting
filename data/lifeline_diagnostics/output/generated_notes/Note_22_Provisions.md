## Note Description:

This note discloses provisions (both current and non-current) recognized in the balance sheet as per Ind AS 37 - Provisions, Contingent Liabilities and Contingent Assets.

### Provisions - Current

**Provision for Income Tax**: ₹61,64,031.00

This includes:
- Creditable EWT (Expanded Withholding Tax): ₹8,89,510.68
- Income Tax Payable: ₹52,74,520.32

Provision for income tax represents the estimated tax liability on taxable income for the current year as per Income Tax Act, 1961. This is the current portion expected to be settled within 12 months from the reporting date. The provision includes:
- Current year income tax liability on taxable profits
- Withholding taxes creditable against final tax liability
- Tax provisions pending final assessment and return filing

The net provision after adjusting creditable taxes represents the amount payable to tax authorities.

### Provisions - Non-Current

**Retirement Benefits Payable**: ₹1,61,90,457.05

Retirement benefits payable represent the non-current portion of employee benefit obligations under Ind AS 19 - Employee Benefits. This provision covers:
- Provision for gratuity (non-current portion)
- Provision for retirement benefits
- Post-employment benefit obligations
- Long-term employee benefits expected to be settled after 12 months

These are provisions for defined benefit plans and other long-term employee benefits expected to be settled after 12 months from the reporting date. The provision is measured based on actuarial valuation as required under Ind AS 19.

---

## Presentation Notes:

1. **Classification**: Provisions are classified as current or non-current based on the expected timing of settlement
2. **Current Provisions**: Expected to be settled within 12 months from the reporting date
3. **Non-Current Provisions**: Expected to be settled after 12 months from the reporting date
4. **Measurement**: 
   - Tax provisions are measured based on applicable tax rates and taxable income
   - Retirement benefits are measured using actuarial valuation techniques
5. **Recognition**: Provisions are recognized when there is a present obligation, probable outflow of resources, and reliable estimate possible
