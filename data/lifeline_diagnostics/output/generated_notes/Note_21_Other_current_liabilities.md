### Notes to the Financial Statements

This note discloses other current liabilities recognized in the balance sheet as per Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013.

**Advances from Customers**: This represents advance payments received from customers amounting to ₹20,28,143.85. This includes:
- Advance payments received from customers for future delivery of goods
- Customer deposits for future services
- Unearned revenue (contract liabilities)
- Deferred revenue from customer advances
- Any other advances received from customers pending delivery of goods/services

**Statutory dues payable**: This comprises various statutory obligations totaling ₹50,71,837.62, which includes:
- Withholding Tax Payable - Compensation: ₹17,09,357.11
- Expanded Withholding Tax Payable: ₹8,17,466.43
- Government Contributions Payable (SSS, PhilHealth, HDMF): ₹23,13,189.04
- Final Withholding Tax Payable: ₹2,31,825.04

These represent amounts deducted from payments to employees and vendors that are payable to government authorities.

**Other Payable**: This represents miscellaneous current liabilities amounting to ₹1,66,004.98, which includes:
- Accounts Payable - Clearing: ₹(1,69,308.55)
- Goods Receipt Not Invoiced: ₹3,303.57

This represents liabilities for goods received but not yet invoiced and clearing accounts for reconciliation.

### Presentation Notes:
1. All items are current liabilities expected to be settled within 12 months from the reporting date
2. All amounts are presented as positive values representing credit balances (liabilities) in the balance sheet
3. These obligations are due to parties other than trade creditors
4. The classification follows Schedule III format for balance sheet presentation under Ind AS
5. Proper classification has been maintained between current and non-current liabilities based on settlement expectations

This note has been prepared in accordance with Schedule III of the Companies Act, 2013 requirements for other current liabilities disclosure under Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS).
